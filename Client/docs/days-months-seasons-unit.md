## Activities Breakdown

### Activity 1: Introduction

**Type**: Introduction
**Purpose**: Welcome learners and introduce the topic
**Content**: "Let's learn how to talk about days, months, and seasons in Swahili!"

### Activity 2: Days of the Week

**Type**: Vocabulary Table
**Content**: All 7 days of the week with Swahili, English, and pronunciation

- Jumatatu (Monday)
- Jumanne (Tuesday)
- Jumatano (Wednesday)
- Alhamisi (Thursday)
- Ijumaa (Friday)
- Jumamosi (Saturday)
- Jumapili (Sunday)

### Activity 3: Months of the Year

**Type**: Vocabulary Table
**Content**: All 12 months with Swahili, English, and pronunciation

- Januari through Desemba

### Activity 4: Seasons

**Type**: Vocabulary Table
**Content**: 4 seasonal terms

- Kiangazi (Summer/Dry Season)
- Masika (Rainy Season)
- Kipupwe (Cool Season)
- Majira (Seasons - general)

### Activity 5: Day Sequencing

**Type**: Multiple Choice
**Question**: "What day comes after 'Jumanne'?"
**Options**: Jumatatu, Jumatano, Alhamisi, Ijumaa
**Correct**: Jumatano (Wednesday)

### Activity 6: Month Recognition

**Type**: Multiple Choice
**Question**: "Which month is 'Machi'?"
**Options**: February, March, April, May
**Correct**: March

### Activity 7: Day Recognition

**Type**: Multiple Choice
**Question**: "What is the Swahili word for Friday?"
**Options**: Jumamosi, Jumapili, Ijumaa, Alhamisi
**Correct**: Ijumaa

### Activity 8: Days Spelling Completion

**Type**: Spelling Completion
**Purpose**: Reinforce spelling of all 7 days
**Items**: 7 days with missing letters

- Example: "Juma_a_u" â†’ Jumatatu

### Activity 9: Months Spelling Completion

**Type**: Spelling Completion
**Purpose**: Reinforce spelling of all 12 months
**Items**: 12 months with missing letters

- Example: "Jan_a_i" â†’ Januari

### Activity 10: Sunday Recognition

**Type**: Multiple Choice
**Question**: "What is 'Jumapili'?"
**Options**: Saturday, Sunday, Friday, Monday
**Correct**: Sunday

### Activity 11: Season Recognition

**Type**: Multiple Choice
**Question**: "Which season is 'Masika'?"
**Options**: Summer, Rainy Season, Cool Season, Winter
**Correct**: Rainy Season

### Activity 12: Days Matching

**Type**: Matching
**Purpose**: Match all 7 days to their English translations
**Pairs**: 7 day pairs (Swahili â†” English)

### Activity 13: Months Matching

**Type**: Matching
**Purpose**: Match 6 months to their English translations
**Pairs**: 6 month pairs (Swahili â†” English)

### Activity 14: Listening Dictation

**Type**: Listening Dictation
**Purpose**: Practice listening comprehension and writing
**Content**: Audio of days and months to transcribe

### Activity 15: Conversation Practice

**Type**: Conversation Practice
**Purpose**: Practice real-world conversations about days
**Dialogue**:

- "Leo ni siku gani?" (What day is it today?)
- "Leo ni Jumatatu" (Today is Monday)
- "Kesho ni siku gani?" (What day is tomorrow?)
- "Kesho ni Jumanne" (Tomorrow is Tuesday)

### Activity 16: Planning Dialogue

**Type**: Dialogue
**Purpose**: Listen to a realistic conversation about planning a meeting
**Participants**: Amina and Juma
**Topic**: Scheduling a meeting on Friday in March
**Dialogue**:

1. Amina: "Tutakutana siku gani?" (What day shall we meet?)
2. Juma: "Tutakutana Ijumaa" (We'll meet on Friday)
3. Amina: "Mwezi gani?" (Which month?)
4. Juma: "Mwezi wa Machi" (The month of March)
5. Amina: "Sawa, nitakuona Ijumaa" (Okay, I'll see you on Friday)
