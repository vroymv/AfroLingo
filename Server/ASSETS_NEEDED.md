## üéØ ASSET CREATION GUIDELINES

### Images

1. **Greeting/Vocabulary Images**: Simple, culturally appropriate illustrations showing the concept
2. **Place Images**: Clear photos or illustrations of each location
3. **Occupation Images**: Professional illustrations of people in their work attire/environment
4. **Color Swatches**: Clean color blocks with optional decorative elements
5. **Recommended Size**: 800x800px or 1200x800px
6. **Format**: PNG with transparency where appropriate
7. **Style**: Consistent, modern, colorful, culturally authentic

### Audio Files

1. **Voice**: Native Swahili speaker (preferably from East Africa)
2. **Quality**: Clear, professional recording (44.1kHz, 128kbps minimum)
3. **Format**: MP3
4. **Length**:
   - Individual words: 2-3 seconds
   - Phrases: 3-5 seconds
   - Dialogues: As needed (typically 30-60 seconds)
5. **Style**: Natural, clear pronunciation at moderate speed
6. **Background**: Silent or very subtle ambient sound
